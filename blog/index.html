The Alchemist

Paulo Coelho's enchanting novel has inspired a generation of readers. This story, dazzling in its powerful simplicity and soul-stirring wisdom, is about an Andalusian shepherd boy named Santiago who travels from Spain to the Egyptian desert in search of a hidden treasure. No matter what your dreams are, The Alchemist is a testimony to the transformative power of our dreams.
